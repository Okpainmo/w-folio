About Me

Passion for catering and my professional occupation as an architect, landed me in such a wonderful occupation combination as I have now.

With over 8 years of experience as an architect, and my passion for "the kitchen" which rubbed on me from my mum, it was very easy to come up with a startup that helps me do all that I love at the same time.
I am an architect who is passionate about aestetics, a "very happy" caterer/events planner, and a professional interior designer/decorator.
I am CEO at Sanders Collectives (a startup with 50 employees - we're one big family), where we put in our passion for beauty to create architectural design, and interior design/decoration master pieces. We also run an awesome event planning/decoration and catering service outfit that keeps producing happy and satisfied clients over and over again.
You can follow this link to contact Sanders collectives, or you can use any of the contact details on this page to reach me.
Cheers!!! and Thanks so much for visiting my website.
